# Rosary
Rosary Mystery Desklet Reminder
I thought it would be a great way for those who pray the Rosary to see a quick visual of the daily Mystery on their screen.
I made this desklet for Linux Mint as a hobby and would like to share with other Christians.

# Installation:
Unzip or extract either the tar.gz file or the zip file to your desktop.
navigate to your desklet folder in user/.local/share/cinnamon/desklets
and place your RosaryMysteries@andres folder into the desklets folder.

# How to use
Right click your desktop and click add desklet
head to the manage tab on top select the Rosary desklet and click the + sign at the bottom of the window.

You should now see your desklet appear.
